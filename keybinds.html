Useful keybinds

below are a list of useful keybinds to know when going into the sever.

General Keybind List

  • H - Whistle Horse
  • U - Horse Interaction Menu
  • E - Get on/off Horse
  • M - Map
  • C - Look behind you
  • X - Raise Hands
  • L - Point
  • B - Inventory
  • Z - See other’s ID’s
  • J - Set Voice Range

Useful commands

  • /loadskin - Fix clothing/body issues
  • /creator - fix your features (does a hard reset)
  • /sleeves - Rolls up sleeves
  • /collar - Rolls up sleeves, unbuttons collar
  • Give Money - Focus on a player then hold L-Shift
  • Hold V - Cinematic Mode
  • Caps Lock - Emotes
  • Tab - Weapon Wheel
  • F6 - Opens Dance Menu
  • /closeinv if the inventory is stuck on your screen
  • /closecatalog if the weapon menu is stuck on your screen
  • /closebank if the bank is menu is stuck on your screen
