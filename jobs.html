All The jobs

Below are a list of jobs that are already in the sever and a short description on each one. However..... You can come up with your own business if you have the imagination and determination to make on e.

Lawmen &Lawwomen

These men and women are players who act as police to the players in the sever. They get a salary for the lenght of time they play. They have the ability to put cuffs onto people, sentance them, give them fines and place them in jail which is called sisika.


Doctors

These players are the medics. They work out of offices in each town and they have powers to heal and revive a player. They also have a salary like the lawmen and women.They can also sell tonics and herbal bandages.


Horse Trainers

These players sell and train horses. They can capture wild horses and sell them off to players. Userally at a great discount to what you will find them at the stables.They can also train up your horse for you so that your horse has the best speed and health for it's breed and coat.


Saloon owners and bar staff

These people are owners of saloon and work the bars around the county. They can serve a list of drinks and food that can be only sold from their establishment.


Lawyers and Judges

These players are in charge of court proceedings. They also help players for things such as name changes, business propolsals, selling of property as well as last wills and testerments.
